Get Started

As you probably know the past several months were crazy for us Amazon sellers, as Amazon went on a hunt and wiped out millions of reviews, blocked seller accounts, products and listing from the ability to gain new reviews. As many other sellers, we were impacted as well by losing more than 900 reviews on our best-selling product! We tried to do everything in our power, contacting Amazon for 2 months on a daily basis, 3rd party consulting and legal services, but unfortunately, we quickly understood this will not resolve our problem. Understanding that, we turned to our tech. department to find a solution within Amazon TOS guidelines, and after a month of hard work, research and dozen successful tests, we were able to find a solution.

Our service, "ASIN DOCTOR" is now running already we have a 100% success rate. We unblock almost any banned to receive new reviews ASIN, allowing you to get within 24H, a brand-new listing page that will allow you to start sell again and collect new reviews without any removals of inventory or any other action on your side! Our service is suitable for those sellers who has vast amount of inventory stuck under a banned asin, their reviews were wiped out + blocked listings and their listing is flagged by Amazon. Our service will save them lots of money and precious coopetition time. Needless to say, it’s impossible to continue competing with the inability to gain new reviews. All negative BSR, review removal & red flags history will be erased, allowing you to start over.

Things To know

  •    Our service is 100% safe and according to Amazon TOS!

  •    We CANNOT reinstate your lost reviews unfortunately, we CAN TRY to save some reviews only in case you have undamaged variations of the same ASIN with reviews.

  •    We take no responsibility on any future new blockages your ASIN will get from Amazon. The only guarantee we give you is that your ASIN will be revived.

  •       You will NOT need to remove any of your inventory, nor change barcodes. Just sit tight and let us do our magic for you and save you time and money!

  •   As you will receive a brand-new listing page, please note your BSR (Best Seller Rank) will be nullified.

  •   The turnaround period of our service varies according to the volume of new orders we get each day and your fast cooperation with our instructions. Your order will be next in line in a first-come-first-served method.

  •   If your product is within one of the banned categories or requires HAZMAT (Hazard Materials) process, an SDS/MSDS/FDA or similar doc., you will need to take care of all processes before we continue with our service.

  •    The process requires some user permissions to your seller central as we do the revive procedure within your “manage inventory dashboard”. Please note, you will grant us ONLY the permissions we must have to revive your ASIN, no confidential info will be revealed to us.
